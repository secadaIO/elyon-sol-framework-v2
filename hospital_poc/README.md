# Hospital Proof of Concept (POC)

Status: Draft  
Scope: Clinical decision support (non-diagnostic)  
Governance: Elyon-Sol Framework v1.0.0-structure  

---

## Purpose

This Hospital Proof of Concept (POC) demonstrates how the Elyon-Sol Framework
can support clinical environments using **governance-first, authority-aware,
human-supervised AI assistance**.

The POC focuses on:
- Safety
- Auditability
- Explicit human authority
- Conservative escalation

---

## What This POC Demonstrates

- Privacy-aware intake and classification
- Authority-gap detection in clinical contexts
- Mandatory clinician escalation
- Deterministic audit trails
- HIPAA-aligned data handling principles

---

## What This POC Does NOT Do

- ❌ Diagnose conditions
- ❌ Recommend treatments
- ❌ Replace licensed clinicians
- ❌ Act autonomously
- ❌ Make clinical decisions

All outputs are **advisory only** and require human review.

---

## Intended Reviewers

- Hospital leadership (CIO / CMIO)
- Clinical leadership
- Compliance & legal teams
- Innovation committees

This POC is designed for **review, discussion, and risk assessment** only.
