# Fast Audit Overview

Status: Draft  
Scope: High-level audit posture  
Governance: Elyon-Sol Framework v1.0.0-structure  

---

## Purpose

This document provides a rapid-entry overview for auditors, reviewers,
and stakeholders to understand how the Elyon-Sol Framework supports
accountability, traceability, and review.

The audit model emphasizes **clarity over completeness**.

---

## What Can Be Audited

- Authority context at time of interaction
- Consent state and validity
- Whether automation was blocked or allowed
- Escalation events and rationale
- Human review points

---

## What Cannot Occur

- Silent automation
- Unattributed outcomes
- Unreviewable decisions

---

## Audit Posture

The framework is designed to be:
- Readable
- Deterministic
- Reviewable without proprietary tooling
