# Limitations

Status: Draft  
Scope: Boundaries of the formal layer  
Governance: Elyon-Sol Framework v1.0.0-structure  

---

## What This Formal Layer Does Not Provide

- Performance guarantees
- Clinical correctness
- Legal correctness
- Automated decision authorization

---

## What This Formal Layer Is For

- Precise definitions
- Reviewable invariants
- Clear separation between:
  - confidence
  - authority
  - consent
  - safety routing

---

## Interpretive Constraint

Any implementation derived from these documents must remain:
- non-autonomous
- authority-supervised
- consent-aware
- auditable
