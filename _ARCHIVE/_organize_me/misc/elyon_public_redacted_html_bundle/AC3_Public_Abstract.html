AC³ — Triadic Collaboration Model (Public Abstract)

AC³ represents a conceptual model for multi-party collaboration between human creators, AI systems, and external verifiers or reviewers. This version omits all operational details and governance mechanics.

1. Purpose

To illustrate how collaborative systems may incorporate multiple perspectives to improve interpretability, accountability, and creativity.

2. Roles (Conceptual Only)

  • A human creator or architect.
  • An AI model acting as a reasoning partner.
  • An external reviewer or verifier.

3. Notes

Internal timing, logic, routing, sovereignty, and system responsibilities are not included in the public edition.
