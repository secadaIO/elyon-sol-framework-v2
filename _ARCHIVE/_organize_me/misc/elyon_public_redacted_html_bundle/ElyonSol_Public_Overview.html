Elyon-Sol — Public Overview

Elyon-Sol is a conceptual exploration into AI governance, ethics, and safer human–AI interaction models. The public edition focuses exclusively on high-level abstractions and does not include internal logic, operational systems, or identity-linked components.

  • Transparency-oriented conceptual AI framework.
  • Focus on human consent, oversight, and ethical alignment.
  • Educational tool for discussing safety-first AI design.
  • Not a production system; no operational capabilities included.

This version is appropriate for public GitHub repositories, presentations, and high-level whitepapers.
