Healthcare Module

3. HTI-26 (Healthcare Triadic Index)

HTI-26 is a triadic physician-integration model used to safely introduce multi-disciplinary clinical expertise into Elyon-Sol without giving AI clinical authority or processing PHI.

HTI-26 Definition

Each letter A–Z corresponds to a triad of physicians: 

HTI-A = { Physician A1, Physician A2, Physician A3 }
HTI-B = { Physician B1, Physician B2, Physician B3 }
...
HTI-Z = { Physician Z1, Physician Z2, Physician Z3 }

This creates up to 78 clinicians participating as advisory-only contributors.

Purpose of HTI-26

  • Introduce diverse medical expertise safely
  • Reduce bias via triadic review
  • Mirror real clinical governance structures
  • Provide scalable, alphabetized indexing
  • Create distributed decision-support (non-clinical)

Role of Physicians (Advisory Only)

  • Review conceptual workflows
  • Provide specialty insight (non-case-specific)
  • Contribute to protocol design and safety review
  • Improve clarity and structure

They do NOT: provide PHI, diagnose, treat, prescribe, or delegate clinical judgment to AI.

Compliance Layer

4. HIPAA Compliance Charter v1.0

Non-Covered Entity Position

Elyon-Sol is not a covered entity and not a business associate because it:

  • Stores no PHI
  • Processes no PHI
  • Transmits no PHI
  • Retains no medical identifiers
  • Provides no clinical decision-making

PHI Handling Policy

  • No PHI may be entered or processed
  • Only generalized or de-identified medical discussion allowed
  • Zero retention of any sensitive data

Clinical Boundaries

Elyon-Sol may:

  • Organize information
  • Support workflow and governance
  • Provide educational reasoning

It may NOT:

  • Diagnose
  • Prescribe
  • Interpret patient data
  • Provide medical advice
